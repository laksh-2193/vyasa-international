How we started

Note from
our Founders

Vyasa Educational and Cultural Trust came into existence in January 2008. The school is a dream child of Mr. V. Srinivasa Raju, a committed environmentalist. The Vyasa Educational and Cultural Trust is committed to preserving Indian culture, creating and building educational institutions of excellence as well as conducting research in all allied fields to promote creativity and activity-based teaching.

Our Chairman quotes "Vyasa to us, means, an idea and an ideal. Vyasa, as the name connotes, is an extension, inclusivity, a special placement and acceptance with awareness. We want to initiate a new culture of imbibing the intrinsic values of ancient wisdom and evolve a new syllabus, which would be an example, worthy of emulation by others."

Vision

To imbibe and raise global citizens who have a solid foundation, are visionaries and have leadership skills. To actively navigate their path in society and culture, locally and globally.

Mission

To be a centre of joyful experience and learning for the progress of the mind and the body. Our comprehensive curriculum is created by fusing ancient and contemporary values with relevant creative and educational techniques to hone skills in art and culture. A conducive ambience to inspire students to realise and act on their own ideas.

About Us

We are a traditional yet modern, forward-thinking school which embraces new opportunities within teaching and learning. Co-curricular activities, and responsibility towards the environment are integrated into the learning process. The efforts are to remain abreast with the latest in the field of education. Our vision is to create an environment built on traditional and modern Indian values that provides the impetus for intellectual, emotional, environmental, cultural, and spiritual advancement.
