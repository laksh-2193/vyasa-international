How we started

Note from
our Founders

Founded in January 2008, Vyasa Educational and Cultural Trust emerged as a visionary initiative spearheaded by Mr. V. Srinivasa Raju, a passionate advocate for environmental causes. At the heart of our mission lies an unwavering commitment to preserving India's rich cultural heritage, establishing world-class educational institutions, and championing research across various domains to encourage innovative and activity-based teaching methodologies.

Our Chairman articulates, "To us, Vyasa embodies both an idea and an ideal. The very name, Vyasa, signifies extension, inclusivity, a distinct positioning, and conscious acceptance. Our mission is to inaugurate a fresh culture that embraces the innate wisdom of the past and crafts a curriculum that sets a remarkable precedent, inspiring others to follow suit."

Commitment to preserving Indian culture, creating and building educational institutions of excellence as well as conducting research in all allied fields to promote creativity and activity-based teaching.

Vision

Our aim is to nurture and empower individuals into becoming global citizens with a strong educational foundation, visionary perspectives, and exemplary leadership abilities. We are dedicated to actively guiding them to navigate and make a positive impact on society and culture, both on a local and global scale.

Mission

We aspire to serve as a hub of joyful experiential learning, fostering the development of both the mind and body. Our holistic curriculum seamlessly blends timeless wisdom with modern insights and utilizes innovative educational methods to cultivate proficiency in art and culture. Our inviting environment is designed to motivate students to explore, embrace, and act upon their unique ideas and passions.

About Us

We are a traditional yet modern, forward-thinking school which embraces new opportunities within teaching and learning. Co-curricular activities, and responsibility towards the environment are integrated into the learning process. The efforts are to remain abreast with the latest in the field of education. Our vision is to create an environment built on traditional and modern Indian values that provides the impetus for intellectual, emotional, environmental, cultural, and spiritual advancement.
